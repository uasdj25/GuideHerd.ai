# GuideHerd Vision

GuideHerd builds AI front-office capabilities for professional-services firms.

The first product is **receptionist-assisted scheduling for law firms**: a
caller reaches the firm, the receptionist qualifies them, and GuideHerd's
Scheduling Assistant books the consultation.

## What we believe

- **The receptionist stays central.** GuideHerd is a tool the receptionist
  hands off to, not a replacement for them. The receptionist keeps judgment,
  relationships, and control of the call.
- **Remove the repetitive work, keep the human judgment.** GuideHerd takes over
  the mechanical parts of scheduling — checking availability, offering times,
  booking, confirming — so staff spend their attention where it matters.
- **The technology stays invisible.** Customers experience GuideHerd. The voice,
  scheduling, calendar, and model providers underneath are never something a
  firm has to think about or choose.

## Where we start

Scheduling is the first module, and it ships complete before we expand. Solving
one real problem exceptionally well earns the right to solve the next one.

## Where it can go

Future capabilities may extend GuideHerd's front office beyond scheduling. These
are directions, not commitments:

- Intake
- Notifications
- Reporting
- Workflow automation
- After-hours support

Each would follow the same rule as scheduling: a GuideHerd capability firms
understand, with the underlying technology kept out of sight.
