# Onboarding Worksheet

Fill this in before kickoff — as a firm, not one person alone. Everything here
is a decision only your firm can make; writing it down first means kickoff is
spent confirming, not dictating. Copy this file, complete it, and share it with
your GuideHerd contact. Where a section points to the guide, the
[Onboarding Guide](onboarding-guide.md) explains the decision and why it
matters.
