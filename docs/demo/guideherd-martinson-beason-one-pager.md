## Header

**GuideHerd**

AI Employees for the work between conversations

*Corner note:* Private demonstration — Martinson & Beason, P.C.

## Headline

The receptionist starts the conversation. GuideHerd finishes it.

## Intro

GuideHerd helps Martinson & Beason carry verified caller information from the
firm's receptionist into the scheduling conversation — without making the
caller start over.

## How the work moves

Human Receptionist → GuideHerd Console → GuideHerd Scheduling Assistant →
Appointment Booked → Consultation Summary

Information is collected once, then carried through the entire scheduling
process.

## What the caller experiences

- No repeated name, email, or case-routing questions
- A smooth transition from the receptionist
- Immediate appointment scheduling
- Clear confirmation of the final appointment

## Benefits for Martinson & Beason

- Less receptionist time spent completing scheduling
- Consistent caller information, with fewer spelling mistakes
- Confirmed scheduling outcomes
- A structured Consultation Summary after the interaction
- Ready for future intake and follow-up automation

## Consultation Summary

GuideHerd automatically prepares a Consultation Summary containing:

- Caller and contact information
- Requested attorney
- Practice area
- Consultation type
- Appointment date and time
- Scheduling outcome
- Follow-up or escalation needs

## Notice what didn't happen

The Scheduling Assistant did not ask the caller to repeat information the
receptionist had already collected. **The human completed the first part of
the work. GuideHerd picked up exactly where the human left off.**

## Where this goes next

The same GuideHerd platform can support additional AI Employees over time:

- GuideHerd Receptionist
- GuideHerd Intake Specialist
- GuideHerd Case Status Assistant
- GuideHerd Billing Assistant
