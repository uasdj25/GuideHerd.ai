# Client Acknowledgment Email Template

> **Demo document.** All content is fictional and used solely for the GuideHerd Legal Intake Copilot demonstration. This template produces DRAFT emails only — no email is ever sent automatically.

---

## Required Formatting

- Every draft must begin with `[DRAFT]` on the first line.
- Every draft must include the "Important Notice" section verbatim or equivalent.
- The attorney must review and edit before sending.

---

## Template

```
[DRAFT] — Requires attorney review before sending. Do not send as-is.

Subject: We Received Your Inquiry — Madison Valley Law Group

Dear [First Name],

Thank you for reaching out to Madison Valley Law Group. We have received your inquiry regarding [brief matter description, e.g., "an estate matter" or "a business dispute"].

We wanted to let you know that we have received your message and an attorney will review your information. If your matter appears to fall within our practice areas and no conflicts are identified, we will be in touch to schedule an initial consultation.

Please note:

- This acknowledgment does not create an attorney-client relationship.
- Submitting this inquiry does not mean the firm has agreed to represent you.
- Before any attorney-client relationship can be formed, the firm must complete a conflict-of-interest review and both parties must sign a written engagement agreement.
- No legal advice is provided by this communication.

If your matter is time-sensitive, please call our office directly at 256-555-0199.

We appreciate your interest in our firm and look forward to speaking with you.

Warm regards,

[Attorney Name]
Madison Valley Law Group
256-555-0199
intake@madisonvalleylaw.example

---
IMPORTANT NOTICE: This email is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. No attorney-client relationship is formed by this communication. Confidentiality of communications is not guaranteed until a formal engagement agreement is in place.
```
