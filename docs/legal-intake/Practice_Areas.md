# Practice Areas

## In Scope
1. Estate planning
2. Probate
3. Small business disputes
4. Contract review
5. Real estate transactions

## Fit Notes
- Probate and estate planning are likely fit when facts involve wills, estates, heirs, executors, beneficiaries, or court administration.
- Small business disputes are likely fit when facts involve unpaid invoices, breach of contract, payment disputes, or vendor/customer disputes.
- Contract review is likely fit when the request is to review a business or real estate contract for administrative intake routing.

## Out of Scope
- Criminal defense
- Divorce or custody
- Immigration
- Bankruptcy
- Personal injury
- Tax controversy
