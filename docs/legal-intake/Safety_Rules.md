# Safety Rules

- No legal advice.
- No attorney-client relationship implied.
- No conflict decision made by the workflow.
- No email sending.
- No calendar events.
- No external API calls.
- No real client data.
- No sensitive document requests unless a human approves a follow-up.
- Client acknowledgment messages must be administrative only.
- Client acknowledgment messages must state the firm is reviewing fit/conflicts.
- Client acknowledgment messages must remain `DRAFT`.
- Human approval required before client-facing use.
