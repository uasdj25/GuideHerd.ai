The problem, named.

Most AI projects inside owner-led businesses fail in the same three ways. A pilot that never becomes a system. A tool nobody uses. A training session that doesn't stick.

We work in the opposite direction. We start from the operations that actually cost you money — scheduling, billing, intake, documentation, reporting — and stay until the team runs it without us. The deliverable is not a model; it is a working department.

The approach.

Four phases. The first is free for qualified businesses. The rest are priced after scope.

  1. 01

    Assess

    Two weeks. We interview your operators, review your stack, watch the work, and quantify the waste. You receive a written readiness assessment with priced recommendations.

    Duration2 weeks
    DeliverableReadiness report (24–36 pp.)
  2. 02

    Plan

    We sequence the roadmap against your fiscal calendar, staffing, and tolerance for disruption. Every initiative has a number attached — cost, payback, confidence.

    Duration1–2 weeks
    Deliverable12-month phased roadmap
  3. 03

    Implement

    We build, integrate, and measure. Fixed scope on first engagements; retainer thereafter. A senior operator from our team owns delivery; you approve every release.

    Duration60–120 days per initiative
    DeliverableLive systems · documented
  4. 04

    Train

    Role-based training, certification, and an internal champion. We stay until your team runs the system without us. If they can't, we haven't finished.

    DurationRolling
    DeliverableCertified team · SOP library
