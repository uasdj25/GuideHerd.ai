This kit gives you four practical tools for mapping and operating your AI-assisted intake workflow. Use the worksheet to design your process, then the three checklists to run it consistently. Print them, copy them into a shared doc, or adapt them into your practice management system.

How to use this kit: Work through the intake workflow worksheet first — it helps you define your firm's specific version of the process. Then use the three checklists as operating tools for each intake you run.

Tool 1 — Intake Workflow Worksheet

Answer these questions to map how AI-assisted intake will work at your firm. There are no universal answers — this is about making the workflow explicit for your context.

Section A — Intake channels

Which channels do prospective clients use to reach your firm? (Check all that apply and note who handles each.)

Section B — Required intake fields

List the information your firm requires before routing a matter internally. These become the checklist for the Missing Information Detector (Prompt 2).

Required fields for our firm:

1. _______________   2. _______________   3. _______________

4. _______________   5. _______________   6. _______________

7. _______________   8. _______________   9. _______________

Section C — Urgency criteria

What makes a matter urgent at your firm? List the signals your team should escalate immediately. These become the context for the Urgency Flagging prompt (Prompt 3).

Our urgency triggers:

1. _______________   2. _______________   3. _______________

Standard follow-up window for non-urgent matters: _______________

Escalation contact for urgent matters: _______________

Section D — Human review assignments

Who is responsible for each review step in your intake process?

Intake summary review: _______________
Missing information follow-up: _______________
Urgency triage decision: _______________
Conflict-screening prep review: _______________
Conflict check (attorney): _______________
Draft acknowledgment approval: _______________
Final send authorization: _______________

Section E — AI tool selection

Which AI tool will your firm use for intake prompts, and has your firm confirmed an appropriate data use policy for that tool?

AI tool selected: _______________
Data policy confirmed: ☐ Yes   ☐ No — do not proceed until confirmed
Confirmed by (name and date): _______________
