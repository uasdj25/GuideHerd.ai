§ FAQ

Common questions

Is starter training included with my implementation?

Yes. Every paid pilot or implementation includes starter training at no additional charge.

How many users are included?

Academy plans include up to 10 users per firm. Contact us if you need more.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans cancel at end of billing period. Annual plans are non-refundable after 14 days.

Is this CLE?

Not by default. GuideHerd Academy is practical workflow training, not CLE unless separately stated.
